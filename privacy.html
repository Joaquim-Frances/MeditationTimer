Privacy Policy for Silence Timer

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

Hiperborean Tech ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Silence Timer mobile application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with global privacy standards, including the Requirements of Google Play's Developer Content Policy.

Privacy Commitment: Silence Timer is built with a "Privacy First" approach. We do not collect any personal, identifiable, or behavioral tracking data beyond the specific anonymous feedback mentioned in this policy.

1. Scope of Data Collection

Silence Timer does not collect any statistics, neither anonymous nor personalized, regarding your general app usage, location, contacts, or device files. We believe your meditation practice is private.

The only data ever collected by the application occurs when you voluntarily participate in an "Update & Feedback" survey.

Data Collected During Surveys

If you choose to complete a survey, we collect:

  • Feature Usage Preferences: Non-personalized checkboxes indicating which app features you find useful (e.g., Timer, Journal).
  • Future Interest: Your votes on potential future features.
  • Technical Context: App version and platform (Android or iOS).
  • Anonymous Identifier: A unique, randomly generated string stored locally on your device to aggregate survey responses without identifying you.
  • User Feedback: Any text comments you explicitly type into our feedback forms.

2. Use of Data

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Understand which features of the application are most valuable to the community.
  • Prioritize our development roadmap based on user interest.
  • Address specific technical issues or suggestions provided in user feedback.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

We use a limited number of third-party services to facilitate the Service:

  • Firebase (Google LLC): We use Firebase Firestore to securely store the anonymous survey data. Google's privacy protocols ensure this data is protected. You can view their policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We use industry-standard encryption provided by Firebase to protect the data during transmission and at rest. Because the data is anonymous, there is no link between the survey results and your real-world identity.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain anonymous feedback data as long as it is relevant for improving the Service. Under Google Play requirements, we provide a mechanism for data deletion. Since the data is anonymous, if you wish to have your survey responses deleted, please contact us with your local 'Anonymous Identifier' (found in app settings) so we can locate your record.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • By email: silence.timer@proton.me
  • By visiting our support section within the application.
